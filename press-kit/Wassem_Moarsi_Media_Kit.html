The short version

Who he is.

Wassem Moarsi owns and operates Sensible Edible Bakery — NYC's largest gluten-free and vegan bakery — in Long Island City. Twenty-plus years in commercial kitchens. BS and MS in biology.

The Wassem Bakes creator brand teaches real techniques to allergen-free bakers: ratios, extraction, gluten behavior, the actual science — not internet folklore. Every recipe he posts has been stress-tested in production across thousands of units a week.

Sensible Edible Bakery is FDA registered and FSMA compliant, operating out of a 100% gluten-free, peanut-free, wheat-free, sesame-free kitchen. The company's guiding principle: "Food should nourish the body, respect animals, and protect the planet." A portion of proceeds supports animal rescue, hunger relief, and environmental restoration.
